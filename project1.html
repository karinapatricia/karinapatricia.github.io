Summer 2020, I worked remotely for OneTrust as sole UX Design intern. I was so thrilled to have been granted the opportunity to work in a team that helped the company become the #1 fastest growing company in the U.S., especially during the pandemic! Going in the program, I was definitely not familiar with the data privacy industry, let alone designing for it, so the knowledge and experience I've gained during the 10-week course was priceless.

Through the course of the internship, my responsibilities included redesigning one of OneTrust's data mapping visualization tools, designing the template editor feature under the software's general settings, and creating cookie banner mockups to present to OneTrust's clients. I learned a lot about really understanding the value in developing a feature, what it means to the target user, and how having these features benefit the company as a whole.

My work is currently under NDA. If you would like to learn more about the work I did at OneTrust and my process, please feel free to email me or shoot a message on LinkedIn!

Thank you OneTrust for an amazing summer!
